### setShallowWaterSimulationFoamAccumulationRate

**Description**

> Sets foam accumulation rate (determines how much and how quickly foam spawns in turbulent waters).

**Definition**

> setShallowWaterSimulationFoamAccumulationRate(entityId shallowWaterSimulation, float foam)

**Arguments**

| entityId | shallowWaterSimulation | id of the shallow water simulation |
|----------|------------------------|------------------------------------|
| float    | foam                   | accumulation rate                  |
