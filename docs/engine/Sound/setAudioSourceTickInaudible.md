### setAudioSourceTickInaudible

**Description**

> Set the inaudible behavior of the sound. By default, if a sound is inaudible, it's paused, and will resume when it
> becomes audible again.

**Definition**

> setAudioSourceTickInaudible(entityId audioSource, boolean tickIfInaudible)

**Arguments**

| entityId | audioSource     | audioSource |
|----------|-----------------|-------------|
| boolean  | tickIfInaudible |             |
