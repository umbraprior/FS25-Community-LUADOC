### getMaterialMetalness

**Description**

> Returns the materials metalness, if available.

**Definition**

> getMaterialMetalness(entityId materialId)

**Arguments**

| entityId | materialId |
|----------|------------|

**Return Values**

| boolean | success   | Indicates whether the returned value is valid |
|---------|-----------|-----------------------------------------------|
| float   | metalness | metalness (0.0 to 1.0)                        |
