### getDensityNormalAtWorldPos

**Description**

> Get normal of the density map at the world position

**Definition**

> getDensityNormalAtWorldPos(entityId fillDataPlaneId, float x, float y, float z)

**Arguments**

| entityId | fillDataPlaneId | fillDataPlaneId |
|----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| float    | x               | x               |
| float    | y               | y               |
| float    | z               | z               |

**Return Values**

| float | nx | normal x |
|-------|----|----------|
| float | ny | normal y |
| float | nz | normal z |
