### calcDistanceFrom

**Description**

> Calculate distance between two nodes

**Definition**

> calcDistanceFrom(entityId transformOne, entityId transformTwo)

**Arguments**

| entityId | transformOne | transformId of first node  |
|----------|--------------|----------------------------|
| entityId | transformTwo | transformId of second node |

**Return Values**

| float | distance | distance between the two nodes |
|-------|----------|--------------------------------|
