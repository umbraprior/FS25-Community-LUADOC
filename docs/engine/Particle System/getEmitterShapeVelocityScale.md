### getEmitterShapeVelocityScale

**Description**

> Returns the emitter shape velocity scale of the particle system
> The particleSystemId can be retrieved by using getGeometry() on the shape/node

**Definition**

> getEmitterShapeVelocityScale(entityId particleSystemId)

**Arguments**

| entityId | particleSystemId | particleSystemId |
|----------|------------------|------------------|

**Return Values**

| float | shape | velocity scale |
|-------|-------|----------------|
