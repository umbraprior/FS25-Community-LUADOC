### getEmitterShape

**Description**

> Returns the emitter shape of the particle system
> The particleSystemId can be retrieved by using getGeometry() on the shape/node

**Definition**

> getEmitterShape(entityId particleSystemId)

**Arguments**

| entityId | particleSystemId | particleSystemId |
|----------|------------------|------------------|

**Return Values**

| integer | shapeId | shapeId |
|---------|---------|---------|
