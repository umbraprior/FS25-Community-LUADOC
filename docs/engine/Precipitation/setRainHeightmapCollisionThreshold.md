### setRainHeightmapCollisionThreshold

**Description**

> Sets a bit of extra height below the actual raycasted rain occlusion that is accepted as an area where rain drops are
> not culled.

**Definition**

> setRainHeightmapCollisionThreshold(entityId precipitationEntity, float tolerance)

**Arguments**

| entityId | precipitationEntity | precipitationEntity |
|----------|---------------------|---------------------|
| float    | tolerance           | tolerance           |
