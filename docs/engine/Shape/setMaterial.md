### setMaterial

**Description**

> Set shape material at index

**Definition**

> setMaterial(entityId shapeId, entityId material, integer materialIndex)

**Arguments**

| entityId | shapeId       | shapeId                                       |
|----------|---------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| entityId | material      | materialId of the material entity             |
| integer  | materialIndex | material index of shape to set, starting at 0 |
