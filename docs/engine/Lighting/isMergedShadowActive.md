### isMergedShadowActive

**Description**

> For the given light source, returns whether the merged shadow (for the entire group of lights merged together) is
> active.

**Definition**

> isMergedShadowActive(entityId lightId)

**Arguments**

| entityId | lightId | id of the light source node |
|----------|---------|-----------------------------|

**Return Values**

| boolean | isActive | true - merged shadows is active. false - merged shadow is inactive |
|---------|----------|--------------------------------------------------------------------|
