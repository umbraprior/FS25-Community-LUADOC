### getShallowWaterSimulationFoamDecayRate

**Description**

> Gets foam decay rate (how quickly foam decays after it has spawned).

**Definition**

> getShallowWaterSimulationFoamDecayRate(entityId shallowWaterSimulation)

**Arguments**

| entityId | shallowWaterSimulation | simulation/id of the shallow water sim to retrieve the texture from |
|----------|------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------|

**Return Values**

| float | foam | decay rate |
|-------|------|------------|
