### getSampleLoopSynthesisStopDuration

**Description**

> Get the loop synthesis sample stop sound duration

**Definition**

> getSampleLoopSynthesisStopDuration(entityId sampleId)

**Arguments**

| entityId | sampleId | sampleId |
|----------|----------|----------|

**Return Values**

| float | stopDuration | duration [ms] of the loop synthesis stop sound. Should be passed to the stopSample delay to make sure the stop sound can be played fully. |
