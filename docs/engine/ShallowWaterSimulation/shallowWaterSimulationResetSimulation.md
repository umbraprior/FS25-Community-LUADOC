### shallowWaterSimulationResetSimulation

**Description**

> Resets simulation to its initial state (water level = determined by the water level planes, all velocities 0)

**Definition**

> shallowWaterSimulationResetSimulation(entityId shallowWaterSimulation)

**Arguments**

| entityId | shallowWaterSimulation | id of the shallow water simulation |
|----------|------------------------|------------------------------------|
