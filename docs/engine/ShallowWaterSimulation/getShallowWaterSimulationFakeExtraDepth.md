### getShallowWaterSimulationFakeExtraDepth

**Description**

> Gets the fake extra terrain height (for terrain under the water) that is used in the simulation, in order to make the
> water deeper than it really is.

**Definition**

> getShallowWaterSimulationFakeExtraDepth(entityId shallowWaterSimulation)

**Arguments**

| entityId | shallowWaterSimulation | simulation/id of the shallow water sim to retrieve the texture from |
|----------|------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------|

**Return Values**

| float | height | extra height added to the water under the rest level |
|-------|--------|------------------------------------------------------|
