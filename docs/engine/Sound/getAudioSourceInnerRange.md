### getAudioSourceInnerRange

**Description**

> Gets inner range of audio source. If the camera is further than this distance volume will start to decrease

**Definition**

> getAudioSourceInnerRange(entityId audioSource)

**Arguments**

| entityId | audioSource | audioSource |
|----------|-------------|-------------|

**Return Values**

| float | range | inner range (fade start) |
|-------|-------|--------------------------|
