### getWorldTranslation

**Description**

> Get translation of a transform object in world space

**Definition**

> getWorldTranslation(entityId transformId)

**Arguments**

| entityId | transformId | transformId |
|----------|-------------|-------------|

**Return Values**

| float | x | x translation |
|-------|---|---------------|
| float | y | y translation |
| float | z | z translation |
