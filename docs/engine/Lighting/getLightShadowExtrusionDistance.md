### getLightShadowExtrusionDistance

**Description**

> Get shadow extrusion distance for light source. This is how far behind the user camera (from the perspective of the
> light source) shadows are still rendered so they can cast shadows into the camera region.

**Definition**

> getLightShadowExtrusionDistance(entityId lightId)

**Arguments**

| entityId | lightId |
|----------|---------|

**Return Values**

| float | extrusion | distance |
|-------|-----------|----------|
