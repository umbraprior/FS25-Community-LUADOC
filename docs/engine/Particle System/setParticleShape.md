### setParticleShape

**Description**

> Sets the particle shape of the particle system
> The particleSystemId can be retrieved by using getGeometry() on the shape/node

**Definition**

> setParticleShape(entityId particleSystemId, entityId nodeId)

**Arguments**

| entityId | particleSystemId | particleSystemId |
|----------|------------------|------------------|
| entityId | nodeId           | nodeId           |
