### setMaxNumOfParticles

**Description**

> The particleSystemId can be retrieved by using getGeometry() on the shape/node

**Definition**

> setMaxNumOfParticles(entityId particleSystemId, integer maxNumParticles)

**Arguments**

| entityId | particleSystemId | particleSystemId                   |
|----------|------------------|------------------------------------|
| integer  | maxNumParticles  | maximum number of active particles |
