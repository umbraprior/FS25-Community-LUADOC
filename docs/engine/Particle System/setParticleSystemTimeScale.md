### setParticleSystemTimeScale

**Description**

> Sets the time scale for the particle simulation.
> The particleSystemId can be retrieved by using getGeometry() on the shape/node

**Definition**

> setParticleSystemTimeScale(entityId particleSystemId, float timeScale)

**Arguments**

| entityId | particleSystemId | particleSystemId |
|----------|------------------|------------------|
| float    | timeScale        | timeScale        |
