### setShallowWaterSimulationFoamDecayRate

**Description**

> Sets foam decay rate (how quickly foam decays after it has spawned).

**Definition**

> setShallowWaterSimulationFoamDecayRate(entityId shallowWaterSimulation, float foam)

**Arguments**

| entityId | shallowWaterSimulation | id of the shallow water simulation |
|----------|------------------------|------------------------------------|
| float    | foam                   | decay rate                         |
