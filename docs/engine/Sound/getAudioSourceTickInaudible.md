### getAudioSourceTickInaudible

**Description**

> Get the inaudible behavior of the sound. By default, if a sound is inaudible, it's paused, and will resume when it
> becomes audible again.

**Definition**

> getAudioSourceTickInaudible(entityId audioSource)

**Arguments**

| entityId | audioSource | audioSource |
|----------|-------------|-------------|

**Return Values**

| boolean | tickIfInaudible |
|---------|-----------------|
