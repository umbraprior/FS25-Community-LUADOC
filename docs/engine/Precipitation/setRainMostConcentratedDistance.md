### setRainMostConcentratedDistance

**Description**

> (Also see setRainDistributionPower). Rain is most densely concentrated at this distance from the camera (in meters).

**Definition**

> setRainMostConcentratedDistance(entityId precipitationEntity, float mostConcentratedRainDistance)

**Arguments**

| entityId | precipitationEntity          | precipitationEntity                                                  |
|----------|------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------|
| float    | mostConcentratedRainDistance | Distance from the camera where the distribution is most concentrated |
