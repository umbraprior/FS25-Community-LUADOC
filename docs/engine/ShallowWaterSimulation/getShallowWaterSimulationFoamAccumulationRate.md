### getShallowWaterSimulationFoamAccumulationRate

**Description**

> Gets foam accumulation rate (determines how much and how quickly foam spawns in turbulent waters).

**Definition**

> getShallowWaterSimulationFoamAccumulationRate(entityId shallowWaterSimulation)

**Arguments**

| entityId | shallowWaterSimulation | simulation/id of the shallow water sim to retrieve the texture from |
|----------|------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------|

**Return Values**

| float | foam | accumulation rate |
|-------|------|-------------------|
