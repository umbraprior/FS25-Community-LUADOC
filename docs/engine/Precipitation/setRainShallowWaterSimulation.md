### setRainShallowWaterSimulation

**Description**

> Sets reference to a shallow water sim for the given rain system. Allows the rain system to use its height map and
> other data in order detect collisions with the water surface.

**Definition**

> setRainShallowWaterSimulation(entityId precipitationGeometry, entityId shallowWaterSim)

**Arguments**

| entityId | precipitationGeometry |
|----------|-----------------------|
| entityId | shallowWaterSim       |
