### shallowWaterSimulationSetNumDepthSources

**Description**

> Sets the number of active depth sources.

**Definition**

> shallowWaterSimulationSetNumDepthSources(entityId shallowWaterSimulation, integer numDepthSource)

**Arguments**

| entityId | shallowWaterSimulation | id of the shallow water simulation |
|----------|------------------------|------------------------------------|
| integer  | numDepthSource         | number of active depth sources     |
