### getChildAt

**Description**

> Get child id at given index. Indices start at 0.

**Definition**

> getChildAt(entityId objectId, integer index)

**Arguments**

| entityId | objectId | objectId |
|----------|----------|----------|
| integer  | index    | index    |

**Return Values**

| entityId | childId | childId |
|----------|---------|---------|
