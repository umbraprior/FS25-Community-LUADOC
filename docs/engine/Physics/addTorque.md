### addTorque

**Description**

> Adds torque to a collision (only for dynamic physics objects)

**Definition**

> addTorque(entityId objectId, float x, float y, float z)

**Arguments**

| entityId | objectId | objectId |
|----------|----------|----------|
| float    | x        | x        |
| float    | y        | y        |
| float    | z        | z        |
