### getMaterialSmoothness

**Description**

> Returns the materials smoothness, if available.

**Definition**

> getMaterialSmoothness(entityId materialId)

**Arguments**

| entityId | materialId |
|----------|------------|

**Return Values**

| boolean | success    | Indicates whether the returned value is valid |
|---------|------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| float   | smoothness | smoothness (0.0 to 1.0)                       |
