### removeMergedShadowIgnoreShapes

**Description**

> (Recursively) removes given entity or its shape children from the ignore list for merged shadow rendering

**Definition**

> removeMergedShadowIgnoreShapes(entityId lightId, entityId shapeId)

**Arguments**

| entityId | lightId | id of the light source node                                        |
|----------|---------|--------------------------------------------------------------------|
| entityId | shapeId | id of shape or parent node of a set of shapes (such as a vehicle). |
