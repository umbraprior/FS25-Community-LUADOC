### getLocalLinearVelocity

**Description**

> Get local linear velocity of transform object (only for kinematic and dynamic physics objects)

**Definition**

> getLocalLinearVelocity(entityId transformId)

**Arguments**

| entityId | transformId | transformId |
|----------|-------------|-------------|

**Return Values**

| float | velocityX | velocityX |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| float | velocityY | velocityY |
| float | velocityZ | velocityZ |
