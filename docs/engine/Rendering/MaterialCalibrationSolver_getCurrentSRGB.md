### MaterialCalibrationSolver:getCurrentSRGB

**Description**

> get currently calibrated color in sRGB format and error value.

**Definition**

> MaterialCalibrationSolver:getCurrentSRGB(integer angle)

**Arguments**

| integer | angle | One of the angle values (-15, 15, 25, 45, 75 or 110) |
|---------|-------|------------------------------------------------------|

**Return Values**

| floatArray | color |
|------------|-------|
| float      | error |
