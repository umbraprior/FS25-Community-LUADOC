### setEmitterShapeVelocityScale

**Description**

> Sets the emitter shape velocity scale of the particle system
> The particleSystemId can be retrieved by using getGeometry() on the shape/node

**Definition**

> setEmitterShapeVelocityScale(entityId particleSystemId, float shape)

**Arguments**

| entityId | particleSystemId | particleSystemId |
|----------|------------------|------------------|
| float    | shape            | velocity scale   |
