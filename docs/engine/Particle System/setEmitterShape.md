### setEmitterShape

**Description**

> Sets the emitter shape of the particle system
> The particleSystemId can be retrieved by using getGeometry() on the shape/node

**Definition**

> setEmitterShape(entityId particleSystemId, entityId shapeId)

**Arguments**

| entityId | particleSystemId | particleSystemId |
|----------|------------------|------------------|
| entityId | shapeId          | shapeId          |
