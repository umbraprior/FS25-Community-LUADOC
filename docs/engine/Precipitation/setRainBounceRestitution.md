### setRainBounceRestitution

**Description**

> Sets the coefficient of restitution for the bounces (essentially, how much a particle slows down upon bouncing).
> Defaultvalue 0.25 (75% slowdown per bounce).

**Definition**

> setRainBounceRestitution(entityId precipitationEntity, float restitution)

**Arguments**

| entityId | precipitationEntity | precipitationEntity        |
|----------|---------------------|----------------------------|
| float    | restitution         | coefficient of restitution |
