### getParticleSystemAverageSpeed

**Description**

> Get particle system average speed.
> The particleSystemId can be retrieved by using getGeometry() on the shape/node

**Definition**

> getParticleSystemAverageSpeed(entityId particleSystemId)

**Arguments**

| entityId | particleSystemId | particleSystemId |
|----------|------------------|------------------|

**Return Values**

| float | normalSpeed  | normalSpeed  |
|-------|--------------|--------------|
| float | tangentSpeed | tangentSpeed |
