### setRainCameraVelocityMultiplier

**Description**

> Set camera velocity multiplier. default 1.0. 0.0 would mean camera velocity is not included in the orientation of rain
> streaks.

**Definition**

> setRainCameraVelocityMultiplier(entityId precipitationEntity, float multiplier)

**Arguments**

| entityId | precipitationEntity | precipitationEntity        |
|----------|---------------------|----------------------------|
| float    | multiplier          | camera velocity multiplier |
